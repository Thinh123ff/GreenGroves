    • Essential Factors for Gardening

    3 important factors to help your small garden grow lush, safe, and effective.

    Suitable Soil & Fertilizer
    • Choose loose, nutrient-rich, well-draining soil.
    • Add organic fertilizer (compost, well-rotted manure) to increase fertility.
    • For small gardens, prioritize clean bagged soil and safe organic fertilizers.
    • Check soil pH (ideal 6.0 - 7.0) for optimal plant growth.
    Safe, Effective Pesticides
    • Prioritize using biological, organic pesticides (neem oil, garlic, chili, etc.).
    • Read instructions carefully, mix correct dosage, spray in the early morning or late afternoon.
    • Wear a mask and gloves when mixing and spraying pesticides.
    • Do not spray close to harvest time, follow the isolation period.
    Seeds, Planting Season & Care
    • Choose quality seeds with clear origin.
    • Sow in the right season (follow package or local instructions).
    • Care: water regularly, fertilize periodically, prune, and check for pests often.
    • Note sowing and care schedule to track growth progress.
